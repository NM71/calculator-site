Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the basic calculator?

Simply click the number buttons to input values, select an operation (+, -, ×, ÷), enter the second number, and press equals (=) to get your result. You can also use your keyboard for faster input.

Does this calculator work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our calculator is fully responsive and optimized for smartphones and tablets. The buttons are touch-friendly and the interface adapts to different screen sizes.

What keyboard shortcuts are available?

Use number keys (0-9) for digits, +/- for addition/subtraction, * for multiplication, / for division, Enter for equals, Escape to clear, and Backspace to delete the last digit.

Is this calculator free to use?

Absolutely! Our basic calculator is completely free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or download needed. Access it anytime through your web browser.

Can I perform calculations with decimal numbers?

Yes, the calculator supports decimal numbers. Click the decimal point (.) button or press the period key on your keyboard to input decimal values for precise calculations.

What's the difference between C and CE buttons?

C (Clear) resets the entire calculator, clearing all numbers and operations. CE (Clear Entry) only clears the current number being displayed, allowing you to correct mistakes without starting over.
