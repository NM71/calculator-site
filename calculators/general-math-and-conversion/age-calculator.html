❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our calculator is extremely accurate, accounting for leap years, varying month lengths, and precise time calculations down to the second. It uses JavaScript's native Date object for maximum precision.

What makes the live age counter special?

The live counter updates every second, showing your age in real-time as it increases. Watch your seconds, minutes, hours, and days lived grow before your eyes - perfect for educational demonstrations or personal fascination with time.

How is the zodiac information calculated?

We calculate both Western zodiac signs (based on birth month/day) and Chinese zodiac animals (based on birth year). The birthstone is determined by birth month, and the life path number uses numerological calculation on your birth date.

Is my birth date information saved?

No! All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't store, save, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.
