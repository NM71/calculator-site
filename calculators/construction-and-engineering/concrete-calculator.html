Concrete Calculator Guide

Concrete Basics

  • 1 cubic yard = 27 cubic feet
  • 80lb bag covers ~0.6 cubic feet
  • 60lb bag covers ~0.45 cubic feet
  • Standard mix: 3000-4000 PSI
  • Curing time: 28 days full strength

Thickness Guidelines

  • Sidewalks: 4 inches
  • Driveways: 4-6 inches
  • Garage floors: 4-6 inches
  • Basement floors: 4 inches
  • Footings: 6-8 inches minimum

Reinforcement

  • Rebar for structural loads
  • Wire mesh for crack control
  • Fiber reinforcement available
  • Expansion joints for large slabs
  • Control joints every 10 feet

Ordering Tips

  • Order 10% extra for waste
  • Minimum delivery: 1 cubic yard
  • Delivery fees typically $75-150
  • Schedule delivery for pour day
  • Have tools ready before delivery
