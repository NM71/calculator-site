Roofing Guide

Shingle Types

  • 3-Tab: Basic, economical option
  • Architectural: Enhanced durability
  • Premium: Highest quality, longest lasting
  • Wood: Natural, requires maintenance
  • Metal: Durable, energy efficient
  • Tile: Long-lasting, heavy material

Roof Pitch Guide

  • Low slope: 2/12 to 4/12
  • Conventional: 4/12 to 9/12
  • High slope: 9/12 and above
  • Steeper = more material needed
  • Affects drainage and snow load
  • Impacts installation difficulty

Coverage Standards

  • 1 Square = 100 square feet
  • 3-Tab: 3 bundles per square
  • Architectural: 3-4 bundles per square
  • Add 10-20% for waste
  • Ridge cap: 35 linear feet per bundle
  • Starter strip: 105 linear feet per bundle

Installation Notes

  • Weather conditions matter
  • Proper ventilation required
  • Safety equipment essential
  • Permits may be required
  • Consider professional installation
  • Warranty varies by material
