Shape Information

2D Shapes (Area)

  • Rectangle: Length × Width
  • Circle: π × radius²
  • Triangle: (Base × Height) ÷ 2

3D Shapes (Volume)

  • Cube: side³
  • Sphere: (4/3) × π × radius³
  • Cylinder: π × radius² × height

Common Units
