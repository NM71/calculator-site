Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Our currency converter uses real-time exchange rates from reliable financial data providers, updated every few minutes to ensure accuracy. However, rates may vary slightly from bank rates due to fees and margins.

Which currencies are supported?

We support 170+ world currencies including all major currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, INR, PKR, JPY, CNY, CAD, AUD, and many more regional currencies.

Is the currency converter free to use?

Yes, our currency converter is completely free to use with no registration required. You can convert between any supported currencies unlimited times.

Why do exchange rates change?

Exchange rates fluctuate due to economic factors like interest rates, inflation, political events, trade balances, and market speculation. These changes happen continuously during market hours.

Can I see historical exchange rates?

Yes, our converter shows historical rate trends for different time periods to help you understand how currencies have performed over time.

What's the difference between mid-market and bank rates?

Mid-market rates are the real exchange rates between currencies. Banks and exchange services typically add margins and fees, so their rates may differ from what you see here.
