Tax Information & Tips

Federal Tax Brackets

The US uses a progressive tax system with rates from 10% to 37%. Your effective rate is typically lower than your marginal rate due to the bracket structure.

State Taxes

State tax rates vary significantly. Some states like Texas and Florida have no state income tax, while others like California have rates up to 13.3%.

Pre-tax Savings

Contributing to 401(k), health insurance, and other pre-tax benefits reduces your taxable income and can significantly lower your tax burden.
