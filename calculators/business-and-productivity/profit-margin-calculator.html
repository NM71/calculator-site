Profit Margin Guide

Gross Margin

Gross margin shows profitability after direct costs. Good margins vary by industry but typically range from 20-50%. Higher margins indicate better pricing power and cost control.

Operating Margin

Operating margin includes all operating expenses. It shows operational efficiency. Healthy operating margins are typically 10-20%, depending on the business model.

Markup vs Margin

Markup is profit over cost, while margin is profit over selling price. A 50% markup equals a 33.3% margin. Understanding both helps with pricing decisions.

Pricing Tips

Consider value-based pricing, competitor analysis, and customer willingness to pay. Factor in fixed costs, market positioning, and long-term business goals.
