Understanding Your Calorie Needs

BMR vs TDEE

BMR is calories burned at rest. TDEE includes activity and exercise. Your goal calories adjust TDEE up or down based on whether you want to gain, lose, or maintain weight.

Weight Management

1 pound of fat equals approximately 3,500 calories. To lose 1 lb/week, create a 500-calorie daily deficit through diet, exercise, or both.

Activity Levels

Choose your activity level honestly. Include both planned exercise and daily activities like walking, cleaning, and occupational movement.

Macronutrients

Protein helps preserve muscle, carbs fuel activity, and fats support hormones. Adjust ratios based on your goals: higher protein for muscle building, moderate carbs for energy.
