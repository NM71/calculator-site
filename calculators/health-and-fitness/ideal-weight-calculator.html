Understanding Ideal Weight Calculations

Multiple Formulas

We use several proven formulas including Hamwi, Devine, Robinson, and Miller methods to provide a comprehensive weight range rather than a single number.

BMI Considerations

While BMI is widely used, it doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition. Our calculator provides context for a more complete picture.

Individual Variation

Ideal weight varies based on genetics, body type, muscle mass, and health conditions. Use these results as a general guideline, not an absolute target.

Health Focus

Focus on overall health, fitness, and how you feel rather than just the number on the scale. Consult healthcare professionals for personalized advice.
